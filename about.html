About Me

I am a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science student at COMSATS University Islamabad. I love web development and I am passionate about helping other beginners learn to code.

I started DebugDiaries because when I was learning HTML and CSS, I struggled to find simple and clear explanations. Every tutorial felt too complicated. So I decided to write the tutorials I wish I had when I was starting out.

On this blog, I write about everything I am learning — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, and more. My goal is to make web development easy and fun for every beginner, especially students like me.

Why DebugDiaries?

Every programmer spends a lot of time debugging — finding and fixing errors in their code. I named this blog DebugDiaries because learning to code is basically a diary of debugging your own understanding, one error at a time.

The name also reminds us that making mistakes is completely normal and part of the process. Every bug you fix makes you a better developer.
